Experience Campfire at your company

Let’s walk through what it’s like to use Campfire at your company. Campfire does the heavy lifting to make the process simple and straightforward.

Curate your bookshelf

Identify the values that represent your company and the culture you want to create. Then, explore our recommendations to find the best books to represent your corporate bookshelf.

Already have a book in mind? We’ll provide content specific to the book you choose that will get your people sharing and discussing with each other.

Identify your champions

Get your leadership team on board and identify the individuals who will lead the culture roll-out.

Reading groups are facilitated by your people. Give your high-potential employees an opportunity to lead.

Set a schedule

Identify an appropriate reading schedule and communicate the schedule with your people. Give space for 15 minutes of reading per day, and set time for regular discussions to talk about the book and share ideas for application.

Receive prompts within Slack to keep everyone on pace and to foster engagment throughout the process.

Keep it fresh and relevant

Keep your company bookshelf visible, and don’t be afraid to change out your books or add new ones. There’s no limit to the number of books that can represent your values.

We’ll take care of your reading needs as your company grows and innovates, while you continue to focus on everything else. :)
