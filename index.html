See how it works

Is your culture ready for the new normal?

As more companies transition to remote work, it is becoming increasingly difficult to collaborate, connect, and solve important problems together. Now is the time to invest in culture.

Leadership

Culture is sparked by leaders and built on teams

Culture isn’t created overnight or influenced by leaders alone. Leaders set direction and support teams in applying values to their work. When the focus is at the team level, culture thrives.

Connection

Remote doesn’t have to mean disconnected

On the scale from strangers-to-family, where is your team? When individuals feel safe and emotionally connected with their team, they are more likely to speak up, share ideas, and make ground-breaking discoveries, even from afar.

Clarity

Get your people on the same page

Gathering for a company retreat is no longer an option. (Thanks, COVID!) We need new ways to get people on the same page. Does your team have a shared vocabulary? When everyone understands and speaks the same language, you move faster.

Skill Development

Invest in your people and they’ll invest in you

Everyone wants to grow and get better. Reading as a team not only builds skills for the future, but it also promotes calm and connection amid uncertain times. When you invest in learning, people will invest their heart into what they do.
